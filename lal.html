Playing: Wuthering Waves

June 30th, 2024

I played the 1.1 story of Wuthering Waves yesterday! I had seen a lot of people online talking about how good it was. And I must say, they were not lying. The storyline was well-paced, the animations were amazing, they had actual cutscenes, the lighting and style design was absolutely amazing and the new zone is beautiful to top it all off. I especially love Jinhsi's background and the fight with Jué. They really upped everything in this patch, listening to player feedback and adjusting their storyline as promised. I seriously can't get over the beautiful animations and cutscenes though. The new zone puzzles and mechanics were also great.
